The CDC provides information on evaluating, testing, managing patients with monkeypox, guidance for health departments on managing monkeypox, laboratory information for testing, and guidance on what to do if someone is sick with monkeypox. They also provide information on safer sex practices to lower the risk of getting monkeypox and how close contacts can protect themselves. They also offer information on when to get tested for monkeypox, what to expect, and photos of monkeypox rash.
